Our Story

Established in 2004 in UAE, Al Manamah has become a leader in Customized Corporate Gifts. We specialize in all brand reminders and exhibition relation items, guiding our clients from ideas to solutions - from concept to mould and final product.

We are experts in the give-away industry including OEM Designs and would be privileged to cater to any of your marketing requirements. You can rely on our dedicated team of experts to suggest the best give-aways that make an impact and create favorable procurement opportunities.

With direct contacts with manufacturers all over the world, we can deliver products directly from the place of origin, ensuring quality and timely delivery.
